# Changelog 0.4.35

## [ADDED]
- Added a **Z-Stack Browser** toggle button (layers icon) to each individual panel in Compare mode, right next to the Grid and Axes buttons.
- **Smart UI visibility**: When the Z-Stack browser is activated on a panel, its UI (the slider and timeline) is automatically hidden when you move your mouse away from that panel. This allows you to see the full, unobstructed slice when looking at other panels, while still keeping the Z-Stack mode active. When you hover back over the panel, the slider reappears instantly.
